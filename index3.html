Problems to solve

Most of the hand therapies for some kids tend to be very monotone and usually is hard to get the kid’s attention in some of the exercises because they do not understand the purpose of them, so we want to solve this problem in order to make easier the therapies for both sides (the kids and the one who does the therapy).

How to solve it

We are creating a functional prototype for a glove with devices in order to make some entertaining ways to stimulate a certain hand movement in this case the movement in the figure 1.1, where the kid will play a game that will interact with his hand to make a dynamic and visual form to develop a movement and at the same time let the user to play and forget he is doing therapy.

Main characteristics

The device will be connected from a leather handheld with three different sensors on the fingerprints, which two of them are flexible resistances that allows to measure the total voltage that is send to an "Intel Galileo Arduino" to see the movement made by the user, the other kind of sensor is capable to measure the pressure that is a fundamental data for the hand pressure rehabilitation and get the feedback necessary for the game to work. All the information gathered in the Galileo Arduino is connected by a micro usb cable to a computer program named processing where the game is being played. The device is easy to carry and adapt to necessities.
